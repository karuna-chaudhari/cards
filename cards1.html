Chilli Paneer

Chilli Paneer is a popular Indo-Chinese dish where cubes of fried crispy paneer are tossed in a spicy sauce made with soy sauce, vinegar, chili sauce. The origin of Chilli Paneer is attributed to an Indian chef Nelson Wang. He is of Chinese origin and the founder of the iconic China Garden in Mumbai's Kemps' Corner. He was the first to use the Indian ingredient paneer and mix it with soy sauce, cornstarch, onions and capsicums.
